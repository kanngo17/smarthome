Overview

The Smart Home Korea includes an interdisciplinary group of faculty, experts, and researchers who are focused on investigating, developing, and evaluating programs to serve the needs of older adults and others with physical and cognitive challenges. This diverse group represents Computer Science, Nursing, Medicine, Social Work, Physical Therapy, and Health Informatics.
